Chapter One

How It Really Happened

(a completely accurate, unbiased retelling)

Twenty years ago, a unicorn wandered up to a pond looking for magic and found a duck instead. The duck looked up and said, "you look like a fruit basket." The unicorn, deeply offended, replied, "it's not a fruit basket, it's a lifestyle."

There was no glitter compatibility test. No mutual friends. No dating app algorithm. Just a horn, some feathers, and two extremely stubborn creatures who both refused to leave the pond first.

For the record: she was the unicorn. Magical, sparkly, mildly terrifying when provoked. I was the duck. Considerably less majestic, but very committed to the pond.

It made no sense on paper. It worked out unreasonably well anyway, and I still haven't found a better pond.

Happy anniversary to my favorite mythical creature. Still picking this pond. Every single time.
